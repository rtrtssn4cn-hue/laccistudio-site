About Lacci Studio

A premium personalization studio built on craftsmanship, meaningful design, and personal connection.

Our Story

Every design tells a story.

Lacci Studio LLC was created from a love of creativity, personalization, and meaningful design. Our purpose is to help customers turn their ideas, photos, artwork, and logos into beautifully personalized products.

Customers may select from designs offered by Lacci Studio or send us their own. Each order is handled with care and close attention to detail, so the finished product feels personal, thoughtful, and unique — pieces you’re proud to use, display, gift, and keep.

The Name

The Story Behind “Lacci”

The name Lacci is deeply personal — inspired by our two Siberian Huskies, Lana and Chibby. We wanted the business to carry a piece of their story without becoming a pet brand.

The beginning of the name comes from Lana. The ending, “cci,” was inspired by Chibby and by the Italian pronunciation of “chi” — which sounds like “kee / cci.” Blended together, they created Lacci: a short, elegant, distinctive name.

Lana’s unique blue-and-brown eyes and Chibby’s personality were part of the original inspiration for the brand — but we intentionally chose not to place dogs in the logo, keeping Lacci Studio refined and versatile for every kind of product and customer.

The Symbol

The Story Behind the Logo

The modern geometric symbol pairs with an elegant wordmark. Its intersecting shapes represent the connection between the customer’s idea, the creative process, skilled craftsmanship, and the finished product.

The symbol also communicates precision, balance, movement, and transformation — the way we bring a design to life. Its clean form works across digital, packaging, stickers, engraving, embroidery, and print.

  • Dark brown — warmth, craftsmanship, stability, and quality
  • Gold — creativity, confidence, and a premium finish
  • Soft ivory — an elegant, welcoming, modern identity
