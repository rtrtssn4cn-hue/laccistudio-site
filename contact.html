Frequently Asked

Can I use my own photo, logo, or artwork? +

Absolutely. You can send your own design, or choose from a design offered by Lacci Studio. Every order is handled with care and close attention to detail.

How does pricing work? +

Because custom-order pricing varies by product, quantity, design, and production requirements, we provide a quote before checkout so you know exactly what to expect.

How do I pay? +

Lacci Studio plans to accept payments online and in person where applicable, including custom-order deposits, invoices, and final balances. We’ll walk you through the options for your order.

What if I’m not sure what I want? +

That’s completely fine — send us your idea and we’ll help you figure out the best product and approach.
