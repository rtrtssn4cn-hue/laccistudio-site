What We Create

Every product is made to order — using one of our designs or your own photo, logo, or artwork. Here’s the full range of what Lacci Studio offers.

Sublimation Printing

Full-color, edge-to-edge printing that bonds directly into the material for vivid, long-lasting results.

Ceramic Coasters

Personalized coasters that pair beautifully with a favorite coffee cup or drink — practical and giftable.

Mouse Pads & Desk Accessories

Custom desk pieces that bring personality and polish to any workspace.

Personalized Socks

Fun, fully-custom socks — a memorable gift or a playful piece of small-business merch.

Tumblers & Drinkware

Sublimated or engraved tumblers, mugs, and bottles designed to be used and treasured daily.

Phone Cases & Magnets

Everyday items made personal — protective phone cases and keepsake magnets from your photos or art.

T-Shirts & Custom Apparel

Shirts and apparel printed or pressed with your design — for gifts, events, teams, and brands.

Stickers, Vinyl & Car Decals

Precision-cut vinyl stickers and decals — durable, weatherproof, and true to your artwork.

Embroidery

Clean, professional embroidery on hats, shirts, and apparel — ideal for logos and monograms.

Laser Engraving

Permanent, detailed engraving on wood and drinkware for a refined, tactile finish.

Personalized Gifts

Thoughtful keepsakes and small-business merchandise, finished with premium presentation.
